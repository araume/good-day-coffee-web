We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you might have.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a coffee recipe?

You can create your own coffee recipe by navigating to the Recipes section and clicking on "Create New Recipe."

How do I access the workbooks?

Workbooks are available in the Workbook section. Click on any workbook to start learning.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes! You can save any recipe to your favorites by clicking the heart icon on the recipe page.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by visiting the Profile section and clicking on "Change Password."
